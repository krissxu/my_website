Zero to One

Back in 2017, I came to the UK alone for studying the foundation courses and undergraduate degree. I didn’t really know anyone here before and my English wasn’t that fluent, which makes the beginning became particularly difficult. (luckily, I soon met my girlfriend here!) And by doing the following things:

  • Always open to make friends with people around.
  • Force myself out of my own comfort zone to talk to new people and try new things.
  • There are no silly questions at univeristy. So never worry about embarrassment.
  • Talk to Uber or taxi drivers. A good way to communicate with native speakers.

In my own way. I successfully managed to adapt to the new environment. Now, I have local volunteer experience. I recently graduate from University of York with the first class and I am studying at London business school! I truely enjoy everying I have experienced and gained so far and I am so looking forward to my brand new start!
